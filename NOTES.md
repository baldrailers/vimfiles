## vinegar/netrw

    -     back
    .     open in command line
    !     open in command line

    D     delete
    d     mkdir
    R     rename
    %     new file

    o     open in horiz split
    v     open in vert split
    t     open in tab

    ^O    back to editing

    i     change view (tree!)

    mf    mark file
    mm    move here

## nerdtree

    ,N    open tree

    o     open
    i     open in split
    t     open in tab
    T     open in tab, silently
